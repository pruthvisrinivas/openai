Whisper


Whisper is a secure messaging protocol developed by OpenAI.
It's designed to enable secure communication between different components in OpenAI's infrastructure while protecting sensitive information.
Whisper uses cryptographic techniques to ensure privacy, authenticity,
and integrity of messages, allowing safe transmission of data within OpenAI's systems.
It's a crucial component in maintaining the security of OpenAI's platforms and services.

  • Secure Communication: Whisper ensures secure communication between various components within OpenAI's infrastructure.

  • Privacy Protection: It employs cryptographic techniques to safeguard sensitive information, ensuring privacy for transmitted data.

  • Authenticity and Integrity: The protocol verifies the authenticity of messages and maintains their integrity throughout transmission.

  • Key Component: Whisper plays a crucial role in maintaining the security and reliability of OpenAI's platforms and services.

  • Internal Infrastructure: It's specifically designed for secure messaging within the internal systems of OpenAI, enhancing overall security measures.

These points highlight Whisper's role in securing communication and data transmission within OpenAI's ecosystem.
